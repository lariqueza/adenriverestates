Dr. Theo Okpidi

Executive Director

Dr Okpidi holds a Phd. In Agronomy and is an expert in soil research and palm oil cultivation. He is a former staff of the Nigerian Institute for Oil Palm Research (NIFOR) and a respected authority on oil palm and modern cropping techniques.

He is a former Chairman of the Oil Palm Company (OPC) at Ajabodudu, Delta State University Investment Company and former head of the pre-degree program of Delta State University (DELSU), Abraka. He is currently in charge of the Delta State Songhai Agricultural Training Programme at Sapele, Delta State.

Friday Adjene

General Manger

Mr Adjene holds a Bsc. Degree in Accounting from the Edo State University, Ekpoma and an MBA from the University of Benin, Benin City. He has also attended the Administrative Staff College of Nigeria (ASCON). He is a member of the Institute of Certified Public Accountants of Nigeria (ICPAN).

Starting as an Assistant Accountant in 1998, Mr Adjene proved his mettle, rising to the position of General Manager within eight years. In his position as the General Manager, he also directly oversees the finance and accounts functions as well as most of the administration and personnel issues.
